This calculator provides estimates based on 2026 VA compensation rates (effective December 1, 2025, 2.8% COLA), CHAMPVA guidelines, Chapter 35 DEA rates, and state-specific benefit data. Actual benefits depend on your specific situation, eligibility requirements, and application status. VA disability compensation is a legal entitlement funded through mandatory spending — there is no cap or limited pool. CHAMPVA catastrophic cap, Chapter 35 DEA rates, and state exemptions may have additional requirements not captured here. Always verify eligibility with your regional VA office or an accredited VSO.
